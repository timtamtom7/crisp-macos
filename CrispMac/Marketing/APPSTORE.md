## Description (App Store)

### What is CrispMac?
CrispMac is a beautifully minimal voice notes app built exclusively for macOS. No typing. No friction. Just open the app, hit record, and speak your thoughts.

### Why CrispMac?
- **Voice-first, always.** Your microphone is the keyboard. Notes are captured, transcribed, and organized automatically.
- **Designed to feel alive.** A real-time waveform breathes with your voice — because recording should be visual, not invisible.
- **Instantly searchable.** Every capture is transcribed and saved. Find what you said with a quick scroll through your library.
- **Keyboard-ready.** A global hotkey puts recording one keystroke away, even when CrispMac is in the background.
- **macOS-native.** Built for Apple Silicon. Runs as a lightweight window or menu bar companion. Dark mode, always.

### Features
- Real-time voice capture with live waveform visualization
- Automatic transcription powered by Apple Speech
- Beautiful, searchable library of all your voice notes
- One-tap edit and copy for any capture
- Audio playback of original recordings
- Clean, distraction-free dark interface

### The Aesthetic
Waveforms. Warm amber tones. A single accent color. Recording that feels like it matters.
